About Us

Consorcio Qutub Minar is an inspection and logistics company. we coordinate, program, control and supervise all the characteristics that must be met in the process of design, manufacturing, testing, dispatching, tracking and reception of materials. with a system that offers predictability and innovation in the logistics process, our team works together across countries, using our network to help our customers improve productivity, reduce costs and achieve success. our quality of services results from our can-do approach and our passion is what sets us apart from other service providers. we are committed to work with efficiency and transparency, acting with integrity in order to earn trust. we strive to maintain a trustworthy relationship, interacting with customers, employees and suppliers in a responsive and rapid way. our values and expertise are the reason why our customers believe in us.
